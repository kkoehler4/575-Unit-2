Data
The data for the interactive map was sourced from the the annual Thanksgving Western Monarch Count, organized by the Xerces Society for Invertebrate Conservation. In this community science effort, a group of volunteers counts Western Monarchs at designated sites along the coast of California. The map displays data from a selection of these sites from 1997 to 2021.

Contextual information used for this website was sourced from Xerces, the Western Monarch Milkweed Mapper, and the National Wildlife Federation.
